Our Story

Crafted, by hand,
for you.

Harper Woolf opened in 2019 with a single idea: an evening that moves at the pace of a long letter.

The kitchen sources from forty smallholdings across Vidzeme and Kurzeme — root vegetables in the cellar through March, langoustine from the Gulf in May, lamb at midsummer. The menu changes when the weather does. The dining room, set beneath vaulted ceilings in the heart of Old Town, holds forty guests for a single, unhurried service.
